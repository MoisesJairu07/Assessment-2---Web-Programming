This Ministry

This website is lovingly created to share the precious Word of God using the King James Version (KJV) Bible. Our mission is to help people discover the glorious truth of salvation through faith in Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior.

Every verse, every word shared here is offered in the hope that it may bring light, comfort, and life-changing truth to all who read it. May God bless you abundantly on your journey of faith.
